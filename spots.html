Public park

Gantry Plaza State Park is a great place for people to come and have all kinds of fun at. The park has many different facitlites such as a large turf field, beach volleyball court, children's playground, basketball court, paths to walk, board walks, etc. The park is right near the water and offers a great view of the city. I personally like to come here to sit on the grass and talk with my friends while eating food or playing a game. If you have friends you should go here.
